About me

I am studying BSc in Computer Science at Ansal Universtiy in Gurgaon. The mediums that I use are mostly oil colors. I also engage in skecthing and make live sketches of nature and people.

My Work usually involves scenes from nature, due to the fact that I try to convey my message with a minimalist's apporach. I try to convey the same emotion in my paintings derived from human expression and emotion. These might involve delight, sadness, anger, confused.
